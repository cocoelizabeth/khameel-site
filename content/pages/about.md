The newborn stage is one of the most beautiful (and most exhausting) seasons of your life. Whether you're a first-time parent or a growing family navigating sleepless nights, having the right support changes everything.

I'm Khameel Simon, a Brooklyn-based Newborn Care Specialist (what many NYC families call a "night nurse" or "baby nurse"). With over 30 years of childcare experience and three years focused exclusively on newborns, I provide calm, professional postpartum support so families can rest, recover, and step into parenthood with confidence.

Whether you need overnight care during the work week or full-time support in the early weeks home, my goal is simple: create a calm, peaceful environment where both baby and parents feel secure.
